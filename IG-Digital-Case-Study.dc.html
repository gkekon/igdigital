THE CHALLENGE

Aurelia had a beautiful product and a forgettable funnel. A premium price tag sat behind a checkout that felt like everyone else’s.

The brand was winning on shelf and losing online. Acquisition costs were climbing, repeat rates were flat, and the site told none of the story that made people fall for Aurelia in the first place. We were brought in to make the digital experience as considered as the formula.
